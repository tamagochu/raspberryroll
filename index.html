Here's how to make the fluffiest raspberry cake roll in the world :)


Ingredients

  • 70g sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 TBSP of hot water

  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 TBSP of sugar
  • 70g flour
  • 1.5 dl whipping cream
  • 1 sachet of vanilla sugar
  • 250g raspberries
  • 2 TBSP sugar

How it's made:

In this recipe, I will show you how to make a raspberry cake roll with my trusty assistant Pochita.


Step 1

The first rule in baking is making a mise en place

This means getting everything measured and ready so you can start baking.

Preheat the oven to 220C

Step 2

Put the egg yolks, sugar and hot water into a bowl and mix until the mixture is foamy

It takes around 5 minutes. It should start having little bubbles.

Something like this!

Step 3

Next, beat the egg whites with the salt until stiff. Add sugar, continue beating until egg whites are glossy.

Don't stop beating until it looks this fluffy! It should stick to the bowl when you flip it over. If its still liquidy, keep beating.

Step 4

Add flour in layers with the beaten egg whites to the mixture, folding in carefully with a rubber scraper.

Don't mix too rough. You want to make sure to not pop all the air bubbles you created by whipping the eggs. Fold it just enough so that it's evenly mixed

Step 5

Carefully spread the dough onto your baking sheet. Bake in the oven for about 7 minutes. Take it out immediately, put another baking sheet on top and flip the tray to let it cool.

You can check if the dough is baked enough with a fork. If nothing sticks to it, you're good :)

Step 6

After the dough is cooled, whip the cream with the vanilla sugar and mash the raspberries with a fork. Mix the raspberries with the sugar

Step 7

Now, spread the whipped cream onto the dough evenly while leaving 2 cm free on the borders. Spread the raspberrries on top of the whipped cream.

Step 8

Roll the cake neatly and carefully, then let it chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.

Finish!!!!
